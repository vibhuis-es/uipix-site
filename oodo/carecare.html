    Car Care Inc.

    Car Repair Work Order Application

    Duration
    8 Weeks
    Platforms
    iPad Application
    Role
    UX Designer

    The Problem

    Senior technicians spend 30-45 minutes per work order typing vehicle details, diagnoses, and customer communications that take them away from actual repair work.


    Research Insights

    Industry Pain Points & UX Opportunities

    Understanding the current challenges faced by automotive technicians and translating them into actionable design opportunities.

    Pain Points

    • Too much manual documentation
    • Technicians spend significant time writing out repair notes and findings

    • Repeating the same information
    • Common issues and repairs are documented from scratch every time

    • Switching between systems
    • Moving between paper forms, desktop software, and digital tools breaks flow

    • Slow customer approvals
    • Waiting for customers to review and approve work creates idle time

    • Parts lookup friction
    • Finding and ordering parts requires multiple steps and system switches

    UX Opportunities

    • Guided workflows
    • Step-by-step flows that reduce decisions and mental load

    • Quick-select inputs
    • Pre-populated options for common repairs, parts, and issues

    • Visual documentation
    • Photo capture and annotation to minimize typing

    • Real-time summaries
    • Auto-generated customer-facing summaries from technician input

    • Digital sign-off
    • In-app approval flow to eliminate delays and paper

    Goals

    • Minimize typing with VIN scan + templates
    • Fast customer sign-off with photos + signature
    • Work offline, sync when connected

    Primary User

    Meet Bob - Senior Technician

    Our primary user persona represents experienced technicians who prioritize efficiency and quality service delivery over learning new technology.

    Design Implication

    Bob represents technicians who need maximum efficiency with minimal learning curve. The interface must feel intuitive from the first interaction, with clear guidance and minimal cognitive load. Success means Bob can complete documentation faster than his current method, without feeling like he's "learning software."

    Core Workflows

    Technician Workflow Overview

    Breaking down Bob's daily workflow into key tasks that the iPad app must support, from initial vehicle inspection through to final billing.


    Design Direction

    UX Design Principles

    Core principles that will guide every design decision, ensuring the app meets the unique needs of technicians working in automotive workshop environments.


    User Journey Mapping

    End-to-End User Journey - From Check-In to Billing

    Mapping Bob's complete experience through a typical work order, identifying pain points at each stage and translating them into concrete UX opportunities.


    Low-Fidelity Wireframes

    iPad Wireframes Flow-Driven UX Structure

    Translating user journeys and workflows into iPad-optimized screen structures. These low-fidelity wireframes focus on information hierarchy, touch interactions, and step-by-step progression before adding visual design.



    Screen 01 - Home / Active Work Orders

    Dashboard showing all active jobs for quick access and status visibility.

    Screen 02 - Vehicle Intake

    Quickly capture vehicle details using VIN scan with auto-fill capabilities.



    Screen 03 - Customer Complaint Capture

    Record initial customer complaint using quick-select or voice input.

    Screen 04 - Technician Diagnosis & Findings

    Document actual problems found using structured fault selection and photo capture.




    Screen 05 - Repair Actions & Parts

    Select repair procedures and required parts with integrated catalog and pricing.


    Screen 06 - Repair Summary & Estimate

    Complete cost breakdown ready for customer review with automatic totals.




    Screen 07 - Customer Approval

    Customer-facing view with digital signature capture for work authorization.


    Screen 08 - Job Completion & Billing

    Final invoice generation and work order closure with automated billing.
