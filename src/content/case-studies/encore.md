## Users & Context

The platform needed to serve multiple roles across the hotel management hierarchy, each with distinct responsibilities and decision-making needs.

### General Managers
**Primary Goals:** Monitor property-level budgets, approve expenses quickly, track monthly performance
**Key Pain Points:** No visibility into real-time spending, approval requests lost in email, manual data compilation for reports

### Regional Managers
**Primary Goals:** Oversee multiple properties, compare performance across hotels, identify budget variances early
**Key Pain Points:** Inconsistent data formats, difficult to spot trends, time-consuming cross-property analysis

### Administrative Teams
**Primary Goals:** Enter budget data accurately, submit expense requests, coordinate vendor payments
**Key Pain Points:** Error-prone spreadsheet entry, unclear approval workflows, fragmented vendor communication

### Vice Presidents
**Primary Goals:** Strategic financial planning, portfolio-wide visibility, make data-driven decisions
**Key Pain Points:** Delayed access to key metrics, no predictive analytics, reliance on manually compiled reports

## Defining the Experience

Through stakeholder interviews and contextual inquiry, we identified that successful financial management in hospitality required role-based access, scenario-driven design, and workflows optimized for speed and clarity.

### Budget Allocation
GMs need to allocate quarterly budgets across departments, view spending trends, and adjust allocations based on seasonality and historical data.

### Expense Approvals
Approval workflows need to be visible, trackable, and fast — with mobile access for managers on the go.

### Monthly & Yearly Analysis
Regional and VP-level users need comparative dashboards with filtering by property, time period, and expense category.
